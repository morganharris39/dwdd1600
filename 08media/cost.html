Cost

There are tons of different materials that go into modding keyboards, and with that, comes varying different costs. Prices can range from $100 to $1000, so knowing what to spend your money on is very important.

Budget Mid-Range Expensive
Materials $20 $30 $40
Case $80-$140 $150-$300 $400-$600
Switches $0.19 per switch $0.26 per switch $0.33 per switch
Keycaps $20-$30 $40-$60 $70-$90
Overall $200 $400 $800
