What are Custom Keyboards?

Custom keyboards are just what they sound like: they're keyboards you can customize! You can customize pretty much everything too; the sound, the feel, the appearance, you can customize all of it! There's pretty much no limit to what you can do. In this guide, I'll be teaching you pretty much everything you need to know the get the exact keyboard you'd like!

What to Expect

In this guide, we'll go over all the elements you need to know to customize your keyboard however you'd like. However, there's a lot of information to cover, which can get pretty overwhelming at times. So, I've created a list of the order in which you should go through the information present on this page below.
